Donna's biography

Donna Sheridan, was born in the UK and followed her love for music to the picturesque Greek island of Kalokairi, where she opened a taverna. She raised her daughter, Sophie, as a single mother while running her business, embodying independence and resilience. Her past entanglements with three men, Sam Carmichael, Bill Anderson, and Harry Bright, resurface when Sophie, seeking her biological father, invites them to her wedding. Donna's life is a tapestry of love and adventure leaving a lasting legacy of warmth and spirit on the island she called home.
